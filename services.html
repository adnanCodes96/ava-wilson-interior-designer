Complete design project

from 4000 rubles / sq.m from 2 months

A detailed guide for the repair and arrangement, following which you will get exactly the interior that you dreamed of. Having a competent design project saves time and effort, helps to avoid unreasonable expenses and immediately achieve the desired result, and not come to it by trial and error.

The complete design project of an apartment is a set of documents necessary for carrying out repair and finishing work at the facility and includes a list of plans in the documentation.

  • Technical task
  • Preliminary design
  • 3D renderings
  • Specification Drawings

    • Equipment

    from 5% of the total project estimate

    Selection and approval of finishing materials, lighting, furniture, plumbing, electrics, comparison of prices, delivery terms, terms and study of reports.

  • Requests for commercial proposals from suppliers of furniture, lighting, sanitary ware.
  • Checking drawings from manufacturers and providing the necessary drawings for them.
  • Organization of ordering and delivery of goods to the site, trips to stores for the selection / approval / purchase of interior items.

    • Decorating

    from 500 rubles / sq.m.

    The selection of decorative elements of the interior is carried out after the completion of the repair and finishing work and includes the selection of accessories, paintings, sculptures, curtains, lighting fixtures, bed linen, bedspreads and cutlery.

  • Development of the concept of decoration in the form of collages.
  • Selection of decor elements (furniture, textiles, finishing materials, lighting, accessories, development of sketches for hand-painted walls).
  • Joint purchase and placement of all decor elements with the customer.

    • Author's supervision

    from 30 000 rub. / month 6 visits

    Control of repair and finishing work to identify inconsistencies between construction work and the project, which includes a visit to the site, consultations of construction teams and making adjustments to working drawings.

    Stages of work
    1. Personal meeting and drawing up a brief.
    2. Departure to the object for professional measurements and photographic recording.
    3. Redevelopment proposal (2-3 options).
    4. Signing a contract and agreeing on the final redevelopment (up to 10 edits).
    5. Stylistic collages for interior decoration. A selection of real interior items reflecting the character and mood of the future interior. Up to 3 options per room.
    6. Creation of 3D visualizations based on approved collages.
    7. Drawing up working documentation for construction work, taking into account the selected and approved equipment.
    Documentation
    1. Measurement plan of the room.
    2. Dismantling plan of walls and partitions.
    3. Furniture layout plan.
    4. Installation plan.
    5. Stylistic collages for interior decoration.
    6. Floor plan indicating the type of material.
    7. Ceiling plan with indication of finishing materials, levels, nodes and sections.
    8. Doorway binding plan.
    9. Plumbing equipment binding plan.
    10. Layout plan for lighting equipment indicating the type of luminaire.
    11. Plan of electrical switches and regulators.
    12. Plan of electrical outlets.
    13. Floor heating plan.
    14. Sweep along the walls of the premises with the layout of the tiles.
    15. Unfolding the walls with the placement of decor items.
    16. Drawings of custom-made doors and furniture.
    17. 3D visualization 2-3 foreshortenings per room.
    18. Material specification for fine finishing
    19. Specification of the offered furniture
    20. Specification of lighting equipment, switches and sockets
