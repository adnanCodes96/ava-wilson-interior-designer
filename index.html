Services

Complete design project

Development of the concept of a beautiful and comfortable interior, taking into account all factors (tastes of the inhabitants, layout and purpose of the premises, budget) and preparation of the regulatory and technical base to translate the idea into reality.

Equipment

The service allows you to get the desired result without spending time and effort on the part of the customer, because selection of furniture, lighting, plumbing and other components by the author of the project guarantees the exact correspondence of the future interior to the developed project and created visualizations.

Decorating

The final stage of the integrated development of interior design, associated with the selection and placement of various decor items and accessories. Thanks to skillful decoration, the interior acquires completeness, becomes harmonious and comfortable.

Author's supervision

Control of repair and finishing works for compliance with the project. Visiting facilities, consulting performers, as well as making changes to the drawings.
